lasagna recipe

DESCRIPTION

delicious layers of pasta,rich meat sauce,creamybaochmel and melted cheese

INGREDIENTS

  • Lasagna noodles
  • ground beef
  • ground meats
  • tomato sauce
  • seasonings and spices etc

STEPS

  1. cook the lasagna noodles
  2. brown the ground beef
  3. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish
  4. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  5. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  6. Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  7. Repeat those layers two times.
  8. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
